APP DEVELOPMENT

App development is the process of creating, designing, and maintaining software applications, which involves several stages from initial idea to post-launch maintenance.

It requires a team with diverse skills, including developers, designers, and testers, and is guided by the purpose of the application and the needs of its target audience.

UI / UX Design

UI (User Interface) design focuses on the look and feel of a digital product's interactive elements, like buttons, icons, and typography

while UX (User Experience) design encompasses the entire user's journey with a product, focusing on its overall functionality and ease of use.
